About Us

Welcome to The Philly Way, your premier platform for discovering the rich history of Philadelphia through an interactive map. Our goal is to immerse you in the historical significance of various landmarks across the city. Whether you're a local resident or a visitor, we aim to educate, inspire, and deepen your connection to the vibrant history that makes Philadelphia unique.

We are a team of four passionate creators, each bringing expertise from the worlds of technology and communications. Together, we are committed to blending technology with education to make Philadelphia’s history accessible and engaging for everyone. Join us as we explore the city’s past, one landmark at a time!
